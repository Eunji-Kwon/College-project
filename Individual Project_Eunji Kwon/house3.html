About This Condo

If you’re looking for a Toronto condo for sale, then Ventus II at Metrogate Condos is a great choice. Standing 30 storeys tall at 181 Village Green Sq, this development provides residents with an excellent starting point to explore the Agincourt neighbourhood. The property was completed in 2012 by Tridel, and is comprised of 314 units, ranging in size from 400 to 921 square feet.



The Neighbourhood

Living here means you’re just a 7-minute walk from Whitesides Terrace Grille, Moti Palace and Thillai Enterprise Inc., making it easy to dazzle your taste buds without much effort. If you enjoy grabbing coffee locally, then you’ll love the fact that you can walk less than 15 minutes to get to Renaissance Garden Cafe, McDonald's and The Best Time CAFE.

There are a number of grocery stores within a short drive, including Foody World, Metro and M&M Food Market.

Planning for your future is easy with Scotiabank and BMO Bank of Montreal in the area.

Getting some fresh air is a breeze when you live at 181 Village Green Sq because excellent green spaces like Metrogate Park and Collingwood Park are walkable in under 8 minutes.

Those who love to shop will appreciate all the options nearby with Work Boots, midtown plaza and Kennedy Commons just 17 minutes from here.

When the weekend rolls around, The Art Forum are within a short 4-minute drive away.

Ventus II at Metrogate Condos is a short drive away from Ellesmere-Statton Public School and Edgewood Public School, giving you plenty of choice for your child’s education.

Transportation

Transit riders can catch the Kennedy Rd At Village Green Sq North Side within minutes. Those who live in the area can easily drive to Midland, just 3 minutes from the building.

In the event that you can’t land a unit in this building, there are some similar condos within walking distance at 151 Village Green Sq, 255 Village Green Sq and 275 Village Green Sq.
